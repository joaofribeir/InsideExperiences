By Train

If you choose the train, come by the Douro line (S. Bento or Campanhã) to Livração. From here to Amarante you will have to make the connection by private car or taxi.

By Bus

The city of Amarante has a bus terminal and the operators are Rodonorte, Valpi, Transdev and Internorte.

By Taxi

During the day you will find taxis in the city center, especially in Largo do Conelheiro António Cândido and in Cândido do Reis Street in front of the Solar de Magalhães.
